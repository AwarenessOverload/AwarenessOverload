--- layout: page-full title: About Us --- Awareness Overload is a fun community website with the goal to seeing a more accessible Ireland. ## Features * Posts and articles to help raise awareness on wheelchair accessibility and disability rights * A map that gives an accurate report on places across Dublin on how wheelchair accessible they are * Regular updates on events or campaigns contributing to disability rights * Fundraising for several charity organizations.
